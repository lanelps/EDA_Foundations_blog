Reflection - Foundations Core Skills

Describe the impact the core learning has had on your Foundations learning journey.

1. What are 1 - 3 new things you have learned about yourself as a result of the core learning? I have learnt;
* That I work well in a pairing program.
* If I keep pushing the idea of a growht mindset I learn faster.
* I am quite empathetic.

2. What has surprised you the most about the core learning?
How implementing these concepts has helped me become a better learner and person. I am more aware of what I am doing at any given moment.

3. What was the most challenging aspects of the core learning?
The most challenging was the reflecting itself and having to write in response to how I think went with certain tasks.

4. Any thoughts you want to share about your core learning journey in Foundations?
Writing is not something I did much of before starting this course. I have found writing has become easier to do the more I reflect.
