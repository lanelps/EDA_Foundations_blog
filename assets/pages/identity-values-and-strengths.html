* Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.


I suppose my culture is New Zealand culture which is all relatively the same throughout the country, altough coming from the south island town of Wanaka, there are certainly some differences in terms of culture in Auckland. North island vs south island, small town vs big city. Coming from Wanaka I was surrounded by nature constantly, it certainly influences how I and my teenage friends behaved having such an open outdoor loving community such as Wanaka. We we're encourgaed to get out and adventure and how could we not in such a place. I'd say if I had my developnig years here in Auckland things were surely be different. This has definitely been a big influencer in my core values.



2. Using the evidence gathered, describe your strengths and limitations.


My strengths I would say are that I'm natrually Curious and Observant. Generally energetic and but mostly always Enthusiastic. I think i'm an excellent communicator. I know how to relax and have fun in certain situations.

My limitations I would say are I find it difficult to focus, I tend to over think things. Poor practicle skills. I can get stressed easily but not outwardly. I tend to be more indepentdent when it comes to projects, and ideas. Putting too much of the role on mysel and not others.

* Identify which of your strengths might help you in your learning journey and how they might intersect with learning obstacles.


I think my enthusiasm and general curiosity will help me with my learning. These strengths usually help me get through the rough parts of learning something new. Keepng optimistic and keen to learn whatever challenges I am facing will always help me pull through.


* Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why.


With my recent graphics course we had to create and brand identity and manage and market our end of year exhibition. We had to do this ontop of our existing projects and deadlines. This was an obviously stresful time for everyone. But I stepped up as one of the lead designers and marketers. The job needed to be done so I took charge. There was some resistance with some descign choices that I and others made from others in the class. The best strategy I found is to sit down and talk about what the issue is and work around and come to a conclusion that everyone could agree on even if it's not 100% what someone had in mind.
