Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset


1. Research Neuroplasticity



- Discuss how understanding the principles of Neuroplasticity benefits people.


Understanding the idea that your brain is always changing and can be changed to form newer and better habits and skills is hugely beneficial to anyone who is trying to learn something new, whether that's a new skill or a way of thinking.



- Discuss how might engage with the principals of Neuroplasticity for your own benefit I will definitely use these ideas and methods of reinforcing good habits by the act of doing.


I can apply this to my studies and my daily fitness and health, if I just practice by doing these beneficial actions my brain will absolutely change for the better.



- What are some of the ways to increase your Neuroplasticity


One way is to just physically do the thing you need to do, form good habits and not reenforcing negative or bad habits. The belief/ the positive outlook that you can indeed change your brain by focusing and doing the right things.

2. Research Growth Mindset



- Discuss what it is and why it is relevant


A growth mindset is knowing that when faced with a challenge you have the ability to learn and understanding that it takes time and practice to overcome. If you don't succeed at them right away know that you can continue to learn and develop the skills necessary to eventually overcome those challenges.



- In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?


Simply people who believe that they cannot improve have a fixed mindset and will not improve their skills while people who have a growth mindset will actually improve their skills if they believe they can eventually improve by working on their skills.



- How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?


I will initiate a growth mindset every time I come across a difficult challenge or find my self trying to tell myself I cannot accomplish something, try and notice that that is happening and remember to keep trying.
