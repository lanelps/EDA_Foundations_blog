EQ vs IQ


What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence aka Emotional quotient (EQ), is a persons ability to recognise, control, express and evaluate their own emotions. They can also empathise with how other people are feeling which is great skill for working with other people.


How is it different to IQ?

Where as Intelligence quotient is a numerical measure of a persons ability to learn and understand logical reasoning, word comprehension, math skills, abstract and spatial thinking.


Why is it important?

By learning how to develop EQ people can hone their emotional understanding, empathy, compassion, reasoning and express them selves emotionally better. This leads to a better life as they can communicate more effectively with the people around them in their life. Wether that be in the workplace, at home or their own friendships.
